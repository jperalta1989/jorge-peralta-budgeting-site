A personal budgeting app that uses Plaid to securely connect financial accounts so the account owner can view balances and transaction history in one place.

Privacy Policy Terms of Use

What the app does

This app is designed for personal budgeting and account review. It helps the account owner bring account balances and transactions into one dashboard for budgeting and cash-flow visibility.

How data is used

The app uses connected account data only to retrieve balances, account details, and transaction history needed for budgeting and financial review by the authorized user.

Support

Data access summary

  • Balances and basic account information
  • Transaction history used for budgeting and spending review
  • Access only with the user’s authorization through Plaid and the connected institution

This site is intended to provide transparency for institutions and users reviewing the app connection.
