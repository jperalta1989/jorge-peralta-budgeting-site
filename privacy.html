Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026

Jorge Peralta Budgeting is a personal budgeting application used to help the authorized user review account balances and transactions in one place.

Information we access

With the user’s permission, the app may access account and transaction data made available through Plaid and the connected financial institution.

How information is used

Information is used only to provide budgeting, spending review, and account-visibility features for the authorized user.

Sharing

Information is not sold. Information is shared only as necessary to operate the connection services requested by the user.

Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect financial data and limit access to authorized use.
